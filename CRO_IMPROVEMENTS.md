# Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Improvements

**Store:** Oil Slick (oil-slick-pad.myshopify.com)
**Date:** 2026-01-24

---

## Problem Identified

The homepage "Best Selling Dab Rigs" featured $8,999 collector pieces as the first shopping experience. This signals "expensive store" before visitors see accessible $25-$150 products.

---

## Merchandising Changes

### New Collections Created

| Collection | Handle | Products | Purpose |
|------------|--------|----------|---------|
| **Value Dab Rigs** | `value-dab-rigs` | 97 | Affordable rigs under $300, sorted by price ascending |
| **Heady Collector Glass** | `heady-collector-glass-1` | 16 | Museum pieces over $500, tucked behind deliberate door |
| **Everyday Essentials** | `everyday-essentials` | 251 | Accessories under $50 - best entry point |
| **Top Packaging Supplies** | `top-packaging-supplies` | 21 | Money categories: jars, PTFE/FEP, parchment |

### Homepage Featured Collections Updated

| Section | Before | After |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Dab Rigs Feature | `dab-rigs` (showed $8,999 items first) | `value-dab-rigs` (starts at $135) |
| Accessories Feature | `accessories` | `everyday-essentials` (under $50) |
| Packaging Feature | `extraction-packaging` | `top-packaging-supplies` |

## Implementation Summary

1. **Two "Front Doors" Created:**
   - Everyday visitors → Value Dab Rigs + Everyday Essentials (accessible prices)
   - Collectors → Heady Collector Glass (deliberate high-ticket section)

2. **Top Sellers Packaging Strip:** Money categories (jars, PTFE/FEP, paper) now featured prominently

3. **Clean SEO Copy:** All keyword-stuffed descriptions replaced with human-readable, conversion-focused copy

4. **Broken Content Removed:** WordPress remnants and internal pages cleaned up

---

## Expected Impact

- **Reduced bounce rate** - visitors see affordable products first
- **Improved SEO** - clean descriptions without keyword penalties
- **Better conversion funnel** - clear paths for different customer types
- **Professional appearance** - no broken shortcodes or placeholder text
