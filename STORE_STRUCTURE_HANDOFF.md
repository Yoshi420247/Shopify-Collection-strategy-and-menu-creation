## STORE OVERVIEW

Oil Slick is a cannabis accessories retailer with two main product lines:

### Vendors
- **Oil Slick** - Original brand specializing in extraction materials and non-stick products
- **What You Need** - Smokeshop/headshop products (glass, vapes, accessories)

### Main Product Categories
1. **Extraction & Packaging** - Silicone pads, FEP/PTFE sheets, glass jars, containers
2. **Smoke & Vape** - Bongs, dab rigs, hand pipes, bubblers, nectar collectors
3. **Accessories** - Quartz bangers, carb caps, dab tools, torches, grinders, papers
